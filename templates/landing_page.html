What is HearMeHearYou?

HearMeHearYou is a virtual ombuds that will be a resource for employees to submit encrypted inquiries privately and/or anonymously. Ombuds is an impartial, confidential resource dedicated to assist individuals to resolve workplace concerns. This will allow employees to seek advice on career development, ethics, workplace harassment, etc.

Why was it created?

We spend the majority of our time at work and 80% of employees lack a trused resource at work. Even if there are resources employees may not feel comfortable using them. HearMeHearYou allows you the option to talk to someone anonymously. This will be a tangible way for companies to show up for employees. The hope is to democratize workplace support so everyone can have an accessible advocate and create a better work environment.

What We Do

Anonymous Reporting

Offer employees a less stressful way to bring up workplace concerns.

Provide advice, prevention strategies and actionable recommendations.
